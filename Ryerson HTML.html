Computer Science (BSc (Hons))

Location

Overview

This program will enable you to become fluent in a plethora of programming
languages. You'll learn about algorithms, data structures, cloud services,
and machine learning technologies and its implementations in the field.
You will also explore topics of web-based development, robotics, and artifical
intelligence. Not only will you have access to research facilities, you will have
the option of devoting one year towards paid co-op/internships.

Program Requirements

Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD) or equivalent with a minimum of six Grade 12 U or M
courses including the following program specific requirements.
  • English/anglais (ENG4U/EAE4U preferred)
  • Advanced Functions (MHF4U)
  • One of Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U) (preferred) or Mathematics of Data Management (MDM4U)
  • One of Physics (SPH4U), Chemistry (SCH4U), Biology (SBI4U)
  • Note: the minimum grade(s) required in the subject prerequisites (normally in the 65-70% range)
    will be determined subject to competition.
  • 82%-90% Average.
